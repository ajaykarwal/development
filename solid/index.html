--- layout: page title: SOLID Principles pre-heading: Ahering to heading: SOLD menu-label: SOLID permalink: /solid/ weight: 1 --- ## Introduction This document is intended as a succinct guide for the Creative Jar development team, encouraging adherence to the SOLID design principles, particularly in the context of a typical piece of software development work undertaken at Creative Jar. A reminder of the SOLID principles: * S - Single Responsibility Principle (SRP) * O - Open Closed Principle (OCP) * L - Liskov Substitution Principle (LSP) * I - Interface Segregation Principle (ISP) * D - Dependency Inversion Principle (DIP)
